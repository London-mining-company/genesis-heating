# EQUIPMENT LEASE & INFRASTRUCTURE PARTNERSHIP AGREEMENT
## GENESIS HEATING SOLUTIONS (PATH 2: "THE BUILDER")

**PARTIES:**
1. **GENESIS HEATING SOLUTIONS** ("Provider")
2. **THE UNDERSIGNED CLIENT** ("Client")

### 1. EQUIPMENT & OWNERSHIP
1.1 Provider shall install one (1) Superheat H1 Decentralized Compute Water Heater (the "Node") at the Client's property.
1.2 **Ownership:** Legal title to the Node remains exclusively with Provider for a period of thirty-six (36) months from the Date of Activation (the "Lease Term").
1.3 **Transfer:** Ownership will transfer to the Client for the sum of $1.00 CAD upon successful completion of Month 36, provided all terms of this Agreement have been met.

### 2. REWARD SHARING & PAYOUTS
2.1 **Structure:** Computed rewards from the Node shall be shared as follows:
   - Months 1-18: Client 40% / Provider 60%
   - Months 19-36: Client 75% / Provider 25%
   - Month 37+: Client 95% / Provider 5% (Management Fee)
2.2 **Volatility Disclosure:** Client acknowledges that compute yields (Bitcoin or otherwise) fluctuate based on network difficulty and market price. Provider makes **zero guarantee** of specific dollar-value returns.
2.3 **Minimum Management Fee:** A monthly fee of $15.00 CAD applies to cover connectivity and health monitoring. If Client's share of rewards is less than $15.00, Client will be billed the difference.

### 3. INSTALLATION & PROPERTY ACCESS
3.1 **Professional Standards:** All installs are performed by Licensed Red-Seal HVAC contractors.
3.2 **Redundancy:** Install must include a plumbing bypass. Provider is not liable for hot water interruption caused by hardware failure of the Node, provided the bypass is functional.
3.3 **Access:** Client grants Provider reasonable access to the property for maintenance or repossession of the Node in the event of default.

### 4. LIMITATION OF LIABILITY & INDEMNIFICATION
4.1 **Property Damage:** While Provider ensures licensed installation, Client agrees that Provider’s total liability for any property damage is limited to the value of Provider's insurance coverage or $10,000 CAD, whichever is higher. Client shall first seek recourse via the Licensed Contractor's bond for installation-related issues.
4.2 **No Profit Guarantee:** Client waives any right to sue for "lost potential earnings" due to network downtime, hardware maintenance, or market volatility.

### 5. DEFAULT & TERMINATION
5.1 **Provider’s Right to Remove:** If Client defaults on payment for more than sixty (60) days, Provider reserves the right to enter the property (during business hours) and remove the Node.
5.2 **Early Buyout:** Client may purchase the unit at any time based on a pro-rated depreciation schedule provided by Genesis.
