# FORREST POOL: REFERRAL PROGRAM TERMS
## GENESIS HEATING SOLUTIONS

**PURPOSE:** To define the incentives for existing Genesis clients who expand the local energy hub in London, Ontario.

### 1. ELIGIBILITY
1.1 **Participant:** Must be an active Genesis Node owner or leaser in good standing.
1.2 **Referral:** A "Referral" is defined as a new client who successfully completes a Genesis install (Package A or B) and pays their initial fee.

### 2. THE REWARD STRUCTURE
2.1 **Cash Reward:** Participant receives a one-time **$100 CAD** credit or cash payout per successful referral.
2.2 **Community Pool Bonus (The "Forest City Hub"):** If three (3) homes in the same postal code primary cluster (e.g., N6G) are active Genesis nodes, all participants in that cluster receive a **5% increase** in their reward-share split for twelve (12) months.

### 3. TERMS & LIMITATIONS
3.1 **Non-Commercial:** The Forrest Pool is for individual residential and small business recommendations. Mass-marketing our referral links on coupon sites is prohibited.
3.2 **Taxation:** Participants are responsible for any personal income tax obligations arising from cash rewards over $500 per year.
3.3 **Changes:** Genesis reserves the right to modify or end the Forrest Pool program with 30 days' notice, though already-earned bonuses will be honored for their duration.
