# HVAC PARTNER MANIFEST (MASTER SUBCONTRACTOR AGREEMENT)
## GENESIS HEATING SOLUTIONS

**PURPOSE:** To define the relationship between Genesis Heating Solutions ("Genesis") and the Licensed HVAC Contractor ("Contractor") for the installation of Superheat H1 Hardware ("The Node").

### 1. CONTRACTOR REQUIREMENTS
1.1 **Licensing:** Contractor must maintain valid Ontario Red-Seal Plumbing/HVAC certifications and TSSA registration.
1.2 **Insurance:** Contractor must maintain at least $2,000,000 in Commercial General Liability (CGL) insurance and provide Genesis with a Certificate of Insurance (COI) naming Genesis as an additional insured.
1.3 **Workers' Comp:** Contractor must be in good standing with the WSIB (Workplace Safety and Insurance Board).

### 2. INSTALLATION PROTOCOL ("THE GENESIS STANDARD")
2.1 **Bypass Requirement:** All installations must include a three-valve plumbing bypass to ensure household hot water continuity during hardware maintenance.
2.2 **The Node Health Check:** Genesis will only release payment to Contractor upon successful digital verification of:
   - Thermal Transfer Rate > 20 gal/hour.
   - Network Signal Stability.
   - High-Resolution Photo Validation (Pipe joins, ESA Permit, Final Aesthetic).
2.3 **Aesthetic Standard:** All wiring and piping must be plumb, level, and secured. Genesis caters to high-end residential clients; "sloppy" installs will be corrected at the Contractor's expense.

### 3. LIABILITY & INDEMNIFICATION
3.1 **Workmanship:** Contractor is solely liable for physical damage to the property (leaks, floods, electrical shorts) arising from their installation work.
3.2 **Indemnification:** Contractor agrees to indemnify and hold Genesis harmless from any claims, suits, or damages arising from Contractor's negligence or faulty workmanship.
3.3 **Existing Conditions:** Contractor must report any pre-existing code violations to Genesis before beginning work.

### 4. PAYMENT TERMS
4.1 **Flat Rate:** Genesis pays a standardized flat rate of **$900 CAD** per standard residential installation.
4.2 **Timing:** Payment via EFT (Electronic Funds Transfer) within 7 business days of a "Verified Node Health Check."
